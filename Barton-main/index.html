Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why should I use Drive Right instead of buying a car myself?

Buying a car through a dealership can be stressful and time-consuming. Most people buy a car every 3 to 5 years, while dealership salespeople sell 3 to 5 cars per day! Your advisor finds the car you want, negotiates on your behalf, and keeps you fully informed. You’ll save time, avoid dealer pressure, and have confidence that you’re getting a fair price.

How much will you save me?

On average, our clients save over $2,400 on their vehicle purchase. We identify hidden fees, bloated add-ons, and inflated markups, and negotiate them away. Often, our savings cover our service fee multiple times over.

How does the trade-in process work?

We evaluate your trade-in value, gather quotes, and maximize your trade-in return in conjunction with your new purchase. You’ll get expert guidance on whether it’s better to trade at a dealer or sell privately, with our team managing negotiations on your behalf.

Can you help with financing or leasing?

Absolutely. We guide you through the financing process to help you secure the best loan or lease terms. We focus on lowering fees, maximizing manufacturer incentives, and finding payment options that fit your budget.

Do you serve all of Texas?

Yes. While we are based in Austin, we serve clients across the entire state of Texas. Whether you are in Dallas, Houston, San Antonio, El Paso, or anywhere in between, we can handle the negotiation and logistics for you. We even handle out-of-state deals and coordinate shipping directly to your door.

Can you help me find a used or certified pre-owned car?

Absolutely. We search across new, used, and certified pre-owned (CPO) inventory from dealerships statewide. For used vehicles, we review vehicle history reports, flag any red flags, and negotiate the best price — so you get the peace of mind that usually only comes with buying new.

How do you get paid?

We charge a flat fee of $495 for our service. We never accept payments or kickbacks from dealers, so our loyalty is entirely to you. We work to help you buy a car, not help the dealer sell one.
