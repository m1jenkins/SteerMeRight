What to Expect

After you reach out, here's how we move forward:

  • A member of our team will respond within one business day to schedule your free 15-minute consultation.
  • During the call, we'll discuss the type of vehicle you're looking for, your budget, and your timeline.
  • You'll receive a clear overview of our flat-fee pricing, no hidden costs and no commission-based incentives.
  • If you decide to move forward, we begin sourcing vehicles, negotiating with dealers, and reporting our findings back to you.
  • Throughout the process, you stay in control. We handle the legwork; you make the final decision.
