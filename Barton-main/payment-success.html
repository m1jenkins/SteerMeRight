Your Car-Buying Journey

Here's exactly how we'll work together to find and secure your perfect vehicle.

  1. Complete Your Car Profile

    Fill out the onboarding questionnaire below so we understand exactly what you're looking for, budget, vehicle type, must-haves, and deal-breakers.

  2. Kickoff Call

    Within 12 hours, your dedicated advisor will reach out to introduce themselves, review your profile, and align on priorities.

  3. Vehicle Search & Shortlist

    We scour dealer inventories, auctions, and private listings across Texas (and beyond) to build a curated shortlist of vehicles that match your criteria.

  4. Negotiation & Deal Protection

    Once you approve a vehicle, we negotiate the best possible price, review every line of the deal sheet, and block hidden fees and unnecessary add-ons.

  5. Drive Away Happy

    We coordinate paperwork, financing review, and delivery logistics. You sign, pick up your car (or we arrange delivery), and enjoy the drive.
