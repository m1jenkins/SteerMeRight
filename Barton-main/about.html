Hi, I'm Mason.

Cars have been a lifelong obsession, not just driving them, but mastering the art of buying them well. Over the years, I've personally bought and sold more than 50 vehicles across the full market spectrum: factory orders at franchised dealerships, private-party transactions, and deeply researched finds sourced from Facebook Marketplace up to 2,000 miles away.

In every single one of those transactions, I paid at or below market value. That discipline has been consistent enough that I've regularly been able to resell my vehicles at a small profit, not from luck, but from a thorough understanding of what a fair deal looks like and a refusal to settle for anything less.

That edge didn't come from a secret. It came from years of research, hundreds of hours of negotiation, and a deep fluency in how the modern car market actually works: dealer margins, manufacturer incentives, regional pricing trends, financing tactics, and every moment where the typical buyer is quietly leaving money on the table.

Now, I'm taking those hard-won skills and making them available to everyone, alongside a team that knows the playbook from the inside.

Dealerships employ trained professionals whose entire job is maximizing what you pay. Most buyers walk in once every few years, unprepared and unfamiliar with the process. We're changing that equation, bringing expertise, data, and negotiating discipline to your side of the table.
